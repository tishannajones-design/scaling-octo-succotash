Typography

Document Typography Note: For printed and Word documents, RGX-Engage uses Source Sans Pro (or Source Sans 3) as the body typeface instead of Inter. Inter remains the UI/web typeface. Montserrat is used for all headings across both contexts.

Brand Voice & Tone

Brand Personality

Information-Intelligent

Deep understanding of how information enables better decisions. Evidence-based, data-driven language. Authority from expertise.

Context-Aware

Solutions matched to organizational maturity. Adaptive language that respects where clients are in their journey.

Execution-Focused

Action-oriented, measurable outcomes. Clear pathways from strategy to results. Concrete, implementation-ready.

Human-Centered

Governance designed for how teams actually work. Accessible, empowering language. User experience prioritized.

Continuously Adaptive

Forward-thinking, evolving approaches. Learning-oriented. Solutions that grow with the business.

Tone: Confident, Clear, Insightful, Energizing

Do

  • Start with the outcome — what will the reader achieve?
  • Use specific, quantified language: "Reduce decision time by 40%"
  • Lead with benefits, support with evidence
  • Be confident without arrogance
  • Use active voice: "Transform governance friction"

Don't

  • Use vague language: "improve efficiency"
  • Say "solutions" without specifics
  • Use "leverage", "synergy", "disruptive"
  • Say "best practices" (implies one-size-fits-all)
  • Use passive voice or corporate jargon without purpose

Quality Checklist

Before Finalizing Any Document

Brand Compliance

  • Montserrat used for all headings
  • Source Sans Pro used for all body text
  • Colors match approved palette exactly
  • RGX-Engage logo on cover page (reports/proposals)
  • No fonts outside Montserrat + Source Sans Pro

Document Structure

  • Cover page present (reports/proposals)
  • Executive summary included
  • Table of contents for 5+ page docs
  • Header present on all pages (except cover)
  • Footer with page numbers (except cover)

Content Quality

  • Tone is confident but not arrogant
  • Language is specific and evidence-based
  • Brand terminology used correctly
  • Active voice used where appropriate
  • Call to action is specific and actionable

Accessibility

  • Heading hierarchy is sequential
  • All images have alt text
  • Sufficient color contrast
  • Minimum 10pt font size
  • No color-only information
